## Sondag 51

**Vraag 126:** Wat is die vyfde bede?
**Antwoord:**
"En vergeef ons ons skulde, soos ons ook ons skuldenaars vergewe" beteken:
Om U ontwil,
moet U ons, arme sondelaars, al ons sondes en ook die boosheid wat nog altyd aan ons kleef, nie toereken nie¹,
net soos ons ook van harte aan ander vergewe².

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: Psalm 51:1-7; 1 Johannes 2:1-2
2: Matteus 6:14-15

## Lord's Day 51

**Question 126:** What is the fifth petition?
**Answer:**
"And forgive us our debts, as we also have forgiven our debtors" means:
For the sake of Christ's blood,
do not impute to us, miserable sinners, any of our transgressions,
nor the evil which still clings to us,¹
as we also find this evidence of Thy grace in us,
that it is our sincere intention to forgive our neighbour heartily.²

**Scripture References:**
1: Psalm 51:1-7; 1 John 2:1-2
2: Matthew 6:14-15
