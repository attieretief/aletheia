## Sondag 43

**Vraag 112:** Wat wil God in die negende gebod hê?
**Antwoord:**
Dat ek teen niemand vals getuienis lewer nie¹,
niemand se woorde verdraai nie²,
geen kwaadprater of kwaadstoker is nie³,
niemand ongevonnisd help veroordeel nie⁴,
maar alle leuens en bedrog as die werke van die duiwel vermy⁵,
tensy ek my 'n swaar straf op die hals wil haal⁶.
Verder dat ek in regspraak en in alle ander sake die waarheid liefhet,
opreg spreek en bely⁷,
en soveel as ek kan, die eer en goeie naam van my naaste verdedig en bevorder⁸.

## Lord's Day 43

**Question 112:** What is required in the ninth commandment?
**Answer:**
That I bear false witness against no one,¹
twist no one's words,²
be no backbiter or slanderer,³
join in condemning no one unheard or rashly;⁴
but that I avoid all lying and deceit as the very works of the devil,⁵
unless I would bring down upon myself the heavy wrath of God;⁶
also that in matters of judgment and justice, and in all other affairs,
I love, speak, and confess the truth honestly;⁷
and that I defend and promote my neighbour's good name as much as I can.⁸
