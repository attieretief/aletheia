## Sondag 1

**Vraag 1:** Wat is jou enigste troos in lewe en in sterwe?
**Antwoord:**
Dat ek met liggaam en siel, in lewe en in sterwe, nie aan myself nie, maar aan my getroue Saligmaker, Jesus Christus, behoort¹.
Hy het met sy kosbare bloed vir al my sondes volkome betaal²
en my uit alle heerskappy van die duiwel verlos³
en so bewaar Hy my,
dat sonder die wil van my hemelse Vader nie een haar van my hoof kan val nie⁴,
ja, dat alle dinge tot my saligheid moet meewerk⁵.
Daarom maak Hy my ook deur sy Heilige Gees van die ewige lewe seker⁶
en Hy maak my van harte gewillig en bereid om voortaan vir Hom te lewe⁷.

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: Romeine 14:7-9; 1 Korintiërs 6:19-20
2: 1 Petrus 1:18-19
3: 1 Johannes 3:8; Johannes 8:34-36
4: Matteus 10:29-31; Lukas 21:18
5: Romeine 8:28
6: 2 Korintiërs 1:21-22; Romeine 8:16-17
7: Romeine 12:1; Lukas 1:74-75

---

**Vraag 2:** Hoeveel dinge moet jy weet om in hierdie troos salig te kan lewe en sterwe?
**Antwoord:**
Drie:
1. Eerstens, hoe groot my sonde en ellende is¹;
2. Tweedens, hoe ek van al my sondes en ellende verlos word²;
3. Derdens, hoe ek God vir so 'n verlossing dankbaar moet wees³.

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: Romeine 3:9-10; Romeine 7:24
2: Johannes 17:3; Romeine 6:23
3: Efesiërs 5:8-10; Romeine 6:1-2

## Lord's Day 1

**Question 1:** What is your only comfort in life and in death?
**Answer:**
That I, with body and soul, both in life and in death, am not my own, but belong to my faithful Saviour Jesus Christ¹.
He has fully paid for all my sins with his precious blood²,
and has set me free from all the power of the devil³.
He also preserves me in such a way
that without the will of my heavenly Father not a hair can fall from my head⁴;
indeed, all things must work together for my salvation⁵.
Therefore, by his Holy Spirit he also assures me of eternal life⁶
and makes me heartily willing and ready from now on to live for him⁷.

**Scripture references:**
1: Romans 14:7-9; 1 Corinthians 6:19-20
2: 1 Peter 1:18-19
3: 1 John 3:8; John 8:34-36
4: Matthew 10:29-31; Luke 21:18
5: Romans 8:28
6: 2 Corinthians 1:21-22; Romans 8:16-17
7: Romans 12:1; Luke 1:74-75

---

**Question 2:** How many things must you know to live and die in the blessedness of this comfort?
**Answer:**
Three:
1. First, how great my sin and misery are¹;
2. Second, how I am delivered from all my sins and misery²;
3. Third, how I am to be thankful to God for such deliverance³.

**Scripture references:**
1: Romans 3:9-10; Romans 7:24
2: John 17:3; Romans 6:23
3: Ephesians 5:8-10; Romans 6:1-2
