## Sondag 13

**Vraag 33:** Waarom word Christus God se eniggebore Seun genoem, terwyl ons ook kinders van God is?
**Antwoord:**
Omdat Christus alleen die ewige, natuurlike Seun van God is¹,
maar ons ter wille van Hom uit genade as kinders van God aangeneem is².

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: Johannes 1:1-3, 14, 18
2: Romeine 8:15-17; Efesiërs 1:5-6

---

**Vraag 34:** Waarom noem jy Hom ons Here?
**Antwoord:**
Omdat Hy ons met liggaam en siel uit die hele ellende,
nie met goud of silwer nie, maar met sy kosbare bloed gekoop het¹
en ons sy eiendom gemaak het².

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: 1 Petrus 1:18-19
2: 1 Korintiërs 6:19-20

## Lord's Day 13

**Question 33:** Why is he called God's "only-begotten Son," when we also are children of God?
**Answer:**
Because Christ alone is the eternal, natural Son of God¹,
but we are children of God by adoption, through grace, for his sake².

**Scripture references:**
1: John 1:1-3, 14, 18
2: Romans 8:15-17; Ephesians 1:5-6

---

**Question 34:** Why do you call him "our Lord"?
**Answer:**
Because he has redeemed us, body and soul, from all our sins,
not with gold or silver, but with his precious blood¹,
and has set us free from all the power of the devil to make us his own possession².

**Scripture references:**
1: 1 Peter 1:18-19
2: 1 Corinthians 6:19-20
