## Sondag 50

**Vraag 125:** Wat is die vierde bede?
**Antwoord:**
"Gee ons vandag ons daaglikse brood" beteken:
Versorg ons hele liggaamlike lewe¹
sodat ons deur dit wat nodig is,
U erken as die enigste bron van alles wat goed is²,
en dat ons sonder ons sorg of ongeregverdigde vertroue op enige skepsel,
ons volle vertroue alleen op U stel³.

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: Psalm 104:27-28
2: Matteus 6:25-34
3: Psalm 55:22; 1 Petrus 5:7

## Lord's Day 50

**Question 125:** What is the fourth petition?
**Answer:**
"Give us this day our daily bread" means:
Provide us with all our bodily needs¹
so that we may acknowledge that Thou art the only source of all that is good,²
and that without Thy blessing neither our care and labour,
nor Thy gifts, can profit us.³
Grant therefore that we may withdraw our trust from all creatures
and place it in Thee alone.³

**Scripture References:**
1: Psalm 104:27-28
2: Matthew 6:25-34
3: Psalm 55:22; 1 Peter 5:7
