## Sondag 49

**Vraag 124:** Wat is die derde bede?
**Antwoord:**
"Laat u wil geskied, soos in die hemel net so ook op die aarde" beteken:
Help ons en alle mense om ons eie wil te verloën¹,
en dat ons sonder enige teëspraak u wil wat alleen goed is, gehoorsaam²,
sodat elkeen sy taak so gewillig en getrou as die engele in die hemel uitvoer³.

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: Matteus 16:24; Lukas 22:42
2: Romeine 12:2
3: Psalm 103:20-21

## Lord's Day 49

**Question 124:** What is the third petition?
**Answer:**
"Thy will be done, on earth as it is in heaven" means:
Grant that we and all men may deny our own will,¹
and without any murmuring obey Thy will, which alone is good,²
so that everyone may carry out his office and calling
as willingly and faithfully as the angels in heaven.³

**Scripture References:**
1: Matthew 16:24; Luke 22:42
2: Romans 12:2
3: Psalm 103:20-21
