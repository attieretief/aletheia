## Sondag 21

**Vraag 54:** Wat glo jy van die heilige, algemene, Christelike kerk?
**Antwoord:**
Dat die Seun van God uit die hele menslike geslag¹,
van die begin van die wêreld af tot aan die einde²,
vir Hom 'n gemeente wat tot die ewige lewe uitverkies is³,
deur sy Gees en Woord⁴ in die een ware geloof⁵
vir Hom vergader, beskerm en onderhou⁶,
en dat ek daarvan 'n lewende lid is en ewig sal bly⁷.

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: Genesis 26:4; Openbaring 5:9
2: Johannes 10:10-11
3: Efesiërs 1:10-13
4: Romeine 10:14-17
5: Efesiërs 4:3-6
6: Psalm 121:4-8
7: 1 Johannes 3:14, 20-21

---

**Vraag 55:** Wat verstaan jy onder "die gemeenskap van die heiliges"?
**Antwoord:**
Eerstens dat die gelowiges almal saam as lede aan die Here Christus en sy skatte en gawes deel het¹.
Tweedens dat elkeen verplig is om sy gawes gewillig en met vreugde tot nut en saligheid van die ander lede aan te wend².

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: Romeine 8:32; 1 Korintiërs 12:12-13
2: 1 Korintiërs 12:21; 13:1-5; Filippense 2:4-6

---

**Vraag 56:** Wat glo jy van "die vergewing van sondes"?
**Antwoord:**
Dat God, ter wille van die versoening wat Christus bewerk het¹,
al my sondes sowel my sondige natuur²,
waarteen ek my hele lewe lank moet stry³,
nie meer wil gedenk nie⁴
en my uit genade die geregtigheid van Christus skenk⁵,
sodat ek nooit weer in sy oordeel kom nie⁶.

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: 1 Johannes 2:2
2: Romeine 7:21-25
3: Galasiërs 5:17
4: Psalm 103:3, 10-12; Jeremia 31:34
5: 2 Korintiërs 5:19, 21
6: Johannes 3:18

## Lord's Day 21

**Question 54:** What do you believe concerning the holy catholic Christian church?
**Answer:**
That the Son of God, out of the whole human race,¹
from the beginning of the world to its end,²
gathers, defends, and preserves for Himself,
by His Spirit and Word,⁴ in the unity of the true faith,⁵
a church chosen to everlasting life;³ ⁶
and that I am and forever shall remain a living member of it.⁷

**Scripture References:**
1: Genesis 26:4; Revelation 5:9
2: John 10:10-11
3: Ephesians 1:10-13
4: Romans 10:14-17
5: Ephesians 4:3-6
6: Psalm 121:4-8
7: 1 John 3:14, 20-21

---

**Question 55:** What do you understand by the communion of saints?
**Answer:**
First, that believers, all and everyone, as members share in Christ and in all His treasures and gifts.¹
Second, that each one ought to know that he is obliged to use his gifts willingly and with joy for the benefit and well-being of the other members.²

**Scripture References:**
1: Romans 8:32; 1 Corinthians 12:12-13
2: 1 Corinthians 12:21; 13:1-5; Philippians 2:4-6

---

**Question 56:** What do you believe concerning the forgiveness of sins?
**Answer:**
That God, because of Christ's satisfaction,¹
will no longer remember my sins² nor my sinful nature,
against which I have to struggle all my life long;³ ⁴
but will graciously grant me the righteousness of Christ,⁵
that I may never come into condemnation.⁶

**Scripture References:**
1: 1 John 2:2
2: Romans 7:21-25
3: Galatians 5:17
4: Psalm 103:3, 10-12; Jeremiah 31:34
5: 2 Corinthians 5:19, 21
6: John 3:18
