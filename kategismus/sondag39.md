## Sondag 39

**Vraag 104:** Wat wil God in die vyfde gebod hê?
**Antwoord:**
Dat ek my vader en moeder en almal wat oor my gestel is, met alle eer, liefde en trou eerbiedig en gehoorsaam¹,
en ook met hulle gebreke geduld het²,
omdat dit God behaag om ons deur hulle hand te regeer³.

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: Efesiërs 6:1-3
2: Spreuke 23:22
3: Kolossense 3:20

## Lord's Day 39

**Question 104:** What does God require in the fifth commandment?
**Answer:**
That I show all honour, love, and faithfulness to my father and mother, and to all those in authority over me,¹
submit myself with due obedience to all their good instruction and correction,²
and also bear patiently with their infirmities,
since it is God's will to govern us by their hand.³

**Scripture References:**
1: Ephesians 6:1–3
2: Proverbs 23:22
3: Colossians 3:20
