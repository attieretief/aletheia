## Sondag 12

**Vraag 31:** Waarom word Hy "Christus", dit is Gesalfde, genoem?
**Antwoord:**
Omdat Hy deur God die Vader verordineer is¹
en met die Heilige Gees gesalf is²
om ons hoogste Profeet en Leraar³,
ons enigste Hoëpriester⁴
en ons ewige Koning⁵ te wees,
daarom:
as Profeet en Leraar het Hy aan ons die hele raad en wil van God oor ons verlossing volkome bekend gemaak⁶;
as Hoëpriester het Hy Homself as 'n eenmalige offer vir ons gegee en ons met die Vader versoen⁷;
en as Koning regeer Hy ons deur sy Woord en Gees,
en beskerm en bewaar ons in die saligheid wat Hy vir ons verwerf het⁸.

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: Hebreërs 1:9; Lukas 4:18
2: Jesaja 61:1
3: Deuteronomium 18:15; Handelinge 3:22
4: Psalm 110:4; Hebreërs 7:17
5: Psalm 2:6; Lukas 1:33
6: Johannes 15:15
7: Hebreërs 9:12, 14
8: Matteus 28:18-20; Johannes 10:28

---

**Vraag 32:** Waarom word jy 'n Christen genoem?
**Antwoord:**
Omdat ek deur die geloof 'n lid van Christus is¹
en so deel het aan sy salwing²,
sodat ek sy Naam kan bely³,
myself as 'n lewende dankoffer aan Hom kan toewy⁴,
en in hierdie lewe teen die sonde en die duiwel met 'n vrye en goeie gewete kan stry⁵
en hierna in ewigheid saam met Hom oor al die skepsels kan regeer⁶.

**Skrifverwysings:**
1: 1 Korintiërs 6:15
2: 1 Johannes 2:27
3: Matteus 10:32
4: Romeine 12:1
5: Efesiërs 6:11; 1 Timoteus 1:18-19
6: 2 Timoteus 2:12

## Lord's Day 12

**Question 31:** Why is he called "Christ," that is, Anointed?
**Answer:**
Because he has been ordained by God the Father¹
and anointed with the Holy Spirit²
to be our chief Prophet and Teacher³,
our only High Priest⁴,
and our eternal King⁵.
As Prophet and Teacher he has fully made known to us the secret counsel and will of God concerning our redemption⁶;
as High Priest he has offered himself as the one sacrifice for us and intercedes for us with the Father⁷;
and as King he governs us by his Word and Spirit,
and defends and preserves us in the salvation he has won for us⁸.

**Scripture references:**
1: Hebrews 1:9; Luke 4:18
2: Isaiah 61:1
3: Deuteronomy 18:15; Acts 3:22
4: Psalm 110:4; Hebrews 7:17
5: Psalm 2:6; Luke 1:33
6: John 15:15
7: Hebrews 9:12, 14
8: Matthew 28:18-20; John 10:28

---

**Question 32:** Why are you called a Christian?
**Answer:**
Because by faith I am a member of Christ¹
and so share in his anointing²,
so that I may confess his name³,
offer myself a living sacrifice of thankfulness to him⁴,
and with a free and good conscience fight against sin and the devil in this life⁵,
and hereafter reign with him eternally over all creatures⁶.

**Scripture references:**
1: 1 Corinthians 6:15
2: 1 John 2:27
3: Matthew 10:32
4: Romans 12:1
5: Ephesians 6:11; 1 Timothy 1:18-19
6: 2 Timothy 2:12
